## 5. Key Insights

### Patient Statistics
- Majority of patients fall within the middle-aged group.
- Certain diagnoses require longer hospital stays.
- Gender distribution is nearly balanced.

### Treatment Outcomes
- Medication-based treatments show higher success rates.
- High treatment cost does not always guarantee success.
- ICU treatments are the most expensive.

### Hospital Metrics
- ICU departments have the highest bed occupancy rates.
- Higher readmission rates are observed in critical care units.
- Departments with lower readmission rates show higher patient satisfaction.
